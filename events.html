Our Events

Annual Literacy Summit

This yearly event brings together teachers, volunteers, and education stakeholders to share innovative literacy strategies and improve reading outcomes for children.

Monthly Teacher Training

A hands-on training session held every month to equip teachers with practical skills and modern techniques for teaching reading and writing effectively.

Weekly Reading & Writing Club

Interactive weekly sessions where children practice reading, writing, storytelling, and comprehension in a fun and supportive environment.

Community Outreach Program

Regular visits to communities to promote literacy awareness, distribute learning materials, and support struggling readers.

Volunteer Training Sessions

Special sessions designed to train volunteers on how to support literacy programs and effectively engage with learners.

Smart Readers Liberia

We are committed to inclusive education and literacy development for children and adults across Liberia.
