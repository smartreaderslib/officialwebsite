DIGITAL SKILLS FOR ALL LEARNERS INITIATIVE

Digital Skills for Every Learner is a digital literacy program under Pillar IX of Smart Readers Liberia, designed to equip learners with essential technology skills needed for education, employment, and everyday life in the modern world. The program recognizes that digital literacy is no longer optional but a fundamental skill for participation in todayâ€™s knowledge-based society. By expanding access to digital skills training, the initiative helps bridge the digital divide and ensures that learners of all ages are prepared for academic success and future career opportunities.

The program is implemented through interactive workshops, online modules, and hands-on training sessions tailored to different age groups and skill levels. Participants learn basic computer operations, internet navigation, digital communication tools, and essential software applications. The initiative also emphasizes digital safety and responsible usage of technology.

Through Digital Skills for Every Learner, Smart Readers Liberia aims to empower learners with the confidence and competence to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. The program fosters innovation, creativity, and critical thinking skills that are essential for lifelong learning and professional development in the 21st century.
