GET TO KNOW ABOUT US!

Our Mission

To provide inclusive literacy and academic support to children, youth, and adults across Liberia, empowering them to become confident readers, lifelong learners, and active contributors to their communities.

Our Vision

A Liberia where every child, youth, and adult has access to quality literacy education and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Story

Smart Readers Liberia, Inc. was founded in February 2021 by passionate educator who recognized the urgent need for literacy support in Liberia. Starting as a small community reading group, we quickly grew into a formal organization dedicated to providing comprehensive literacy programs for learners of all ages. Our journey has been driven by a commitment to inclusive education and a belief that literacy is a fundamental right that can transform lives and communities. Today, we serve hundreds of learners across Liberia, helping them build strong reading skills, gain confidence, and achieve better educational outcomes. Our work is made possible by a dedicated team of staff and volunteers who share our vision for a more literate and empowered Liberia. Learn more about our programs
