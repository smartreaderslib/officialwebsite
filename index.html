Founder & Executive Director

Welcome Message

You're warmly Welcome to Smart Readers Liberia, Inc.

At Smart Readers Liberia, we believe that every child, regardless of background, social status, and abilities deserves the opportunity to read, write, and rise. Access to literacy skill should not be a previlege, it is a right. It is not just a skill; it is also the foundation for lifelong learning, confidence, and meaningful participation in society. Therefore, every child needs to start right!

Since our establishment in 2021, we have remained committed to supporting children with diverse learning needs, especially those who struggle with reading. Through our flagship Children’s Literacy Development Program, we provide targeted support that helps young learners build strong reading and writing skills. Learn More.

Beyond this, our work has expanded to include teacher training and volunteer development, where we equip educators with structured, innovative, and research-based instructional strategies to better support struggling readers in their classrooms or communities Learn More.

We are also proud to offer adult literacy programs, ensuring that learning is not only limited to a centain age group, but to individuals of all ages. And as part of our broader strategy to improve exam scores for senior secondary candidates, we offer remedial education support for students preparing for or re-sitting the West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE) and the West African Examinations Council (WAEC) exams, helping them improve performance and regain academic confidence. Learn More.

In addition, our "College Preparation and Career Development" initiatives guide senior secondary students and first-year college students toward brighter futures by equipping them with essential academic and life skills such as time management, notes taking strategies, exam preparation, test or exam taking strategies, financial literacy skills among others Learn More.

At Smart Readers Liberia, our mission goes beyond teaching reading, we are nurturing confident learners, critical thinkers, and future leaders who will contribute meaningfully to Liberia and beyond. We also work closely with parents and communities to promote a culture of reading and lifelong learning.

As Founder, I invite you to join us, whether as a volunteer, partner, donor, or advocate. Together, we can transform lives, promote inclusive learning for lifelong success.

Please contact us to learn more about how you can get involved. Additionally, please take up some time to explore our website to learn more about our initiatives.

Thank you for believing in our mission.
