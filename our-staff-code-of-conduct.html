OUR STAFF CODE OF CONDUCT

At Smart Readers Liberia, Inc., we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and respect in all aspects of our work. Our Staff Code of Conduct outlines the principles and expectations that guide our interactions with learners, colleagues, partners, and the communities we serve. This code is designed to foster a positive and inclusive work environment, promote ethical behavior, and ensure that our staff members uphold the values and mission of our organization. By adhering to this code, we aim to create a culture of trust, accountability, and excellence in our efforts to support literacy and education in Liberia.

  • 1. Professionalism:

    • Staff members are expected to conduct themselves in a professional manner, demonstrating respect, courtesy, and competence in all interactions.

  • 2. Integrity:

    • We uphold honesty and transparency in our work, ensuring that all actions align with our organizational values and ethical standards.

  • 3. Respect for Learners:

    • Our staff members are committed to treating all learners with dignity and respect, fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

  • 4. Confidentiality:

    • Staff members must maintain the confidentiality of sensitive information related to learners, colleagues, and organizational operations, sharing information only when necessary and appropriate.

  • 5. Collaboration:

    • We encourage teamwork and collaboration among staff members, recognizing that our collective efforts are essential to achieving our mission and maximizing our impact.

  • 6. Continuous Learning:

    • Staff members are encouraged to pursue ongoing professional development and stay informed about best practices in education, literacy, and organizational management.

  • 7. Cultural Sensitivity:

    • We respect and value the diverse cultural backgrounds of our learners and communities, ensuring that our programs and interactions are culturally appropriate and inclusive.

  • 8. Lifelong Learning:

    • We promote continuous learning and personal growth for individuals of all ages. Our Core Values
