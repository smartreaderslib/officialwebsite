Student Pre-Registration Form

This pre-registration form is designed to help Smart Readers Liberia, Inc. identify learners who are interested in our literacy and academic support programs. By completing the form, students or parents can share basic information and select one preferred program of interest. Submitting this form does not guarantee immediate enrollment, but it allows our team to plan effectively, communicate next steps, and ensure each learner receives the appropriate support.
