OUR PROGRAMS

At Smart Readers Liberia, our work is guided by nine interconnected pillars that reflect our commitment to building a literate, skilled, and socially responsible society. These pillars represent a holistic approach to education—one that recognizes literacy as the foundation for personal growth, academic success, and national development. From early childhood through adulthood, our programs are designed to meet learners at every stage of life, ensuring that no one is left behind because of age, disability, or socioeconomic circumstance.

Each pillar is implemented through a targeted program that responds to Liberia’s real educational and social needs. We focus on strengthening children’s literacy, expanding lifelong learning opportunities, promoting inclusive education for the visually impaired, and supporting learners who need academic second chances. Beyond basic literacy, our pillars extend to college and career readiness, professional skills development, and continuous teacher professional development—equipping both learners and educators with the tools they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Recognizing that education goes beyond the classroom, Smart Readers Liberia also integrates environmental awareness and digital literacy into its core pillars. By promoting clean and healthy school environments and equipping learners with essential digital skills, we prepare individuals to be informed citizens, responsible environmental stewards, and confident participants in the global knowledge economy. Together, our nine pillars and programs form a strong framework for empowering individuals, strengthening communities, and advancing sustainable development across Liberia.
